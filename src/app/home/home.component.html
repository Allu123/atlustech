IRS Form 2290, Heavy Vehicle Used Tax (HVUT ) is a Federal Excise Tax that is imposed on heavy weight highway vehicles with gross weight of 55,000 pounds or more. Owners of the heavy weight vehicles must file the Heavy vehicle use tax annually through filing 2290 form online and get their Stamped Schedule 1. This Tax is applicable for certain heavy taxable vehicles such as tractor, trucks, trailers and buses which use public highways.

E-filing Form 2290 must be done during the tax period of July 1 to June 30 every year. And the deadline of filing form 2290 is August 31. If the due date falls on weekends, then the tax payer can file the form 2290 by next business day. File your Form 2290 online and save your time.
